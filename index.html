Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons (November-February). However, we sometimes have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to reach out even if your date is coming up soon!

What's included in your same-day reel service?

Our same-day reel service includes 2-3 professionally edited Instagram reels (30-60 seconds each) delivered on the same day as your event. We'll capture all the key moments and have them ready for you to share with friends and family by the end of your event.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely! We love destination weddings. Travel expenses (flights, accommodation) will be added to the package cost. Contact us with your location, and we'll provide a customized quote.

How long does it take to receive our full wedding video?

Our standard delivery time for the full wedding film is 4-6 weeks. We'll send you a highlight reel within 2 weeks, and the complete edited footage will follow. For pre-wedding shoots, delivery is typically 2-3 weeks.

What's your payment and cancellation policy?

We require a 30% deposit to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 7 days before your event. Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event receive a full refund. For cancellations within 30 days, the deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a rescheduled date within 6 months.

Can we request specific songs for our wedding video?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share your favorite songs or any special music that's meaningful to you. We'll work with you to select the perfect tracks that fit the mood and style of your video. Just keep in mind that for public sharing, we'll need to use royalty-free or licensed music.
